Online Installation

Mike Taanila

Committed to recuperating the consumer ideologue of progress and technology, Mika Taanila is the celluloid-wielding soothsayer of Nordic moving image; a key proponent and unparalleled forebear of ‘post-cinema’.

With an erudite singularity, fields and themes are navigated freely: implacable scientific advance, nuclear power, man as machine (mastering / malfunctioning), parallax perspectives, failed—and possible—utopias; the future past . . . Taanila’s visionary art visits London at a critical juncture in the culture, and the times, in what will be the most complete international survey of his practice to date.
